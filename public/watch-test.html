Watch not showing in the chooser?
1. Close the watch's own app (QCBand / Colmi / H Band / Da Fit) on your phone — while it's connected there, the watch stops advertising to anything else.
2. Forget the watch in your phone's system Bluetooth settings, then retry.
3. Make sure the watch screen is on (tap it) so it's actively advertising.
4. On Android Chrome, enable chrome://flags/#enable-web-bluetooth-new-permissions-backend if devices still don't appear.
5. Try "Scan watches only" below — it filters by common watch name prefixes which sometimes surfaces devices that "Scan all" misses.
