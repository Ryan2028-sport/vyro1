Live decoder · first-byte streams (move your wrist!)
Buckets every notification by its first byte and decodes the 14 payload bytes as 6× int16 (LE and BE). If these numbers wiggle when you shake the watch, that stream is raw IMU. If they only change when you press buttons or stay static while moving, it's just status/historical data.
