Court geometry · idle baseline · live T-return counter
How it works: we use your watch's wrist-motion signal (0x69). When you're standing at the T, motion is low. When you leave to chase a ball, it spikes. When you come back, it drops. Each low → high → low cycle = 1 T-return. Calibration just samples your baseline so the threshold fits your wrist.
